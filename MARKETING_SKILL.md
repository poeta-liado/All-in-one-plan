# Marketing CRM AI Assistant: How to Acquire 30 Shops in 1 Year

**Target**: Acquire 30 shops/businesses as customers within 12 months  
**Constraints**: Limited network and budget  
**Product**: CRM AI Assistant (automated customer relationship management with AI capabilities)

---

## QUICK CHECKLIST (Weekly Marketing Tasks)

- [ ] **Social Media** (2x per week)
  - Post product tip or use case on LinkedIn/Twitter
  - Engage with 10+ posts from target audience
  - Share customer success story or demo

- [ ] **Direct Outreach** (3-5 prospects)
  - Identify 3-5 shop owners/managers in your target market
  - Send personalized email with specific value prop
  - Follow up with warm introduction if possible

- [ ] **Content** (1-2 pieces per week)
  - Create 1 blog post, video, or case study
  - Repurpose into social media snippets
  - Track engagement metrics

- [ ] **Measurement** (Weekly review)
  - Track new leads/inquiries
  - Update conversion metrics
  - Identify what's working/what's not

- [ ] **Relationship Building** (ongoing)
  - Reply to all comments/DMs within 24 hours
  - Schedule 1-2 customer calls/demos
  - Ask existing customers for referrals

## DETAILED MARKETING WORKFLOW

### Phase 1: Product Positioning & Messaging (Weeks 1-2)

**Goal**: Define how you talk about your CRM AI Assistant

1. **Identify Your Ideal Customer (ICP)**
   - Who benefits most? (small retail, service, e-commerce shops)
   - What's their main pain point? (customer data, follow-up, retention)
   - What size business? (1-50 employees)
   - What's their budget range?

2. **Craft Core Messages**
   - **Problem Statement**: "Shops lose track of customer preferences and follow-up opportunities"
   - **Solution**: "AI-powered CRM that automatically learns customer patterns and suggests next actions"
   - **Key Benefits** (pick 3):
     - Saves 5+ hours/week on manual CRM entry
     - Increases repeat customer rate by X%
     - Never miss a follow-up opportunity
   - **Unique Angle**: What makes this different from Salesforce/HubSpot for small shops?

3. **Create 3 Customer Personas**
   - Persona A: Shop owner managing 5-10 staff
   - Persona B: Marketing manager at retail chain
   - Persona C: E-commerce founder with high churn
   - Write 1 paragraph about each person's daily challenges

**Deliverable**: 1-page positioning doc + 3 customer personas

---

### Phase 2: Build Zero-Cost Marketing Engine (Weeks 2-4)

**Strategy**: Leverage free channels to build credibility without budget

#### A. Content Marketing (Blog/Video)
1. **Topics** (pick 5 for first month):
   - "5 Reasons Shops Lose Customers (and how AI fixes it)"
   - "How to 10x your repeat customer rate"
   - "CRM tips for small retail businesses"
   - Case study: "How [Shop Name] increased revenue 20%"
   - "AI tools for customer retention: pros and cons"

2. **Production Format** (choose 2-3):
   - Blog posts (1,500-2,000 words, SEO optimized)
   - Video shorts (60-90 sec demo clips)
   - LinkedIn articles (professional insights)
   - Twitter threads (tips/insights)

3. **Repurposing**: Each piece becomes:
   - 1 LinkedIn post
   - 2-3 Twitter/X posts
   - 1 email segment
   - 1 snippet for Instagram/TikTok

#### B. Social Media Strategy
1. **LinkedIn** (Primary - B2B/shop owners)
   - Post 3-4x per week
   - Content mix: 50% tips, 30% demos, 20% thought leadership
   - Engage daily with target audience posts
   - Join 3 relevant groups (retail, e-commerce, small business)

2. **Twitter/X** (Secondary - thought leadership)
   - Post 2x per week
   - Reply to industry conversations
   - Share real-time product updates

3. **Instagram/TikTok** (Optional but powerful for reaching shop staff)
   - Short product demos
   - "A day with CRM AI Assistant" behind-the-scenes
   - User tips and tricks

#### C. Email List Building
1. **Create lead magnet** (free resource):
   - Template: "Customer Retention Checklist for Shops"
   - Or: "CRM Setup Guide for Small Businesses"
   - Or: "AI Marketing Prompts for Shop Owners"

2. **Landing page** (simple):
   - One-pager on your website or Notion
   - Headline, 3 benefits, email form, thank you

3. **Email sequence** (nurture):
   - Welcome email
   - 3x valuable tips (spaced 3 days apart)
   - Soft CTA: "Schedule 15-min demo"

**Deliverable**: 5 content pieces + active social accounts + lead magnet

---

### Phase 3: Targeted Outreach (Weeks 4-26)

**Goal**: Directly reach ideal customers (constraint-friendly - personal touches over paid ads)

#### A. Identify Prospects
1. **Research sources** (free):
   - LinkedIn Sales Navigator (or free LinkedIn search)
   - Local business directories (Chamber of Commerce, industry listings)
   - Google Maps: "shops near [city]", "retail stores", "service businesses"
   - Industry-specific directories

2. **Create prospect list**:
   - Target 30 shops total for the year
   - Track in simple spreadsheet: Name, Email, LinkedIn, Industry, Status
   - Aim for 2-3 new prospects per week

3. **Prioritize by warmth**:
   - Tier 1 (Warm): Referrals, mutual connections, events
   - Tier 2 (Warm-ish): People who've engaged with your content
   - Tier 3 (Cold): Direct research, no prior contact

#### B. Outreach Campaign
1. **Tier 1 (Warm Introductions)**
   - Ask mutual connection for intro
   - Email: Personal 2-3 sentence mention + intro
   - Follow-up: Demo offer

2. **Tier 2 (Warm-ish)**
   - Reference their post/business
   - Email subject: Personalized (NOT generic)
   - Body: Specific problem they likely face
   - CTA: "Chat for 15 min?"

3. **Tier 3 (Cold Outreach)**
   - Research their business first
   - Email subject: Value-focused, not pitch
   - Show you understand their business
   - Example: "3 ways AI CRM saves [their industry] time"

#### C. Email Template (Customize for each)
```
Subject: [Personalized reference to their business]

Hi [Name],

[Relevant observation about their business]

I work with [similar type business] who want to [solve problem they face].
We built an AI CRM that [specific benefit].

[Social proof]: [Shop name] saved 5+ hours/week on customer follow-up.

Would you be open to a quick 15-min call to see if it makes sense?

[Your name]
```

#### D. Follow-up Sequence
- Email 1 (Day 1): Initial outreach
- Email 2 (Day 5): Light follow-up ("checking in")
- Email 3 (Day 12): Different angle or case study
- Email 4 (Day 21): Final attempt with clear CTA
- Stop after 4 touches or positive response

**Deliverable**: Prospect list + outreach templates + tracked conversations

---

### Phase 4: Demo & Conversion (Ongoing)

**Goal**: Turn leads into customers

1. **Demo Strategy**:
   - Focus on THEIR pain: How does CRM AI solve their specific problem?
   - Show real data: Pull a sample from their industry
   - Interactive: Let them try a feature
   - Time: 20-30 minutes max

2. **Conversion Offers**:
   - First month free (risk reversal)
   - Setup assistance included
   - 30-day money-back guarantee
   - Referral incentive: Discount for each referred shop

3. **Sales Materials**:
   - 1-page product overview (easy to email)
   - ROI calculator (show savings)
   - Pricing page (clear and simple)
   - FAQ addressing common objections

**Deliverable**: Demo script + sales enablement materials

---

### Phase 5: Customer Retention & Referrals (Month 1+)

**Goal**: Keep customers and turn them into advocates

1. **Onboarding**:
   - Welcome call (explain features, answer questions)
   - Setup assistance (import data, configure)
   - First month check-in (ensure they see value)

2. **Retention**:
   - Monthly check-in calls
   - Educational content (webinars, tips)
   - Community space (Slack, Discord, or forum)
   - Celebrate their wins (case studies, testimonials)

3. **Referral Program**:
   - Incentive: $100 credit or discount for each referral
   - Simple one-page explainer
   - Give-away metrics: Track referral sources

**Deliverable**: Onboarding checklist + referral tracking
